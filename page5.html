About Our Organization

Our NGO, HSE Organization, is a student-led initiative dedicated to creating a better world through tree-planting, social welfare, and animal welfare initiatives. Our team is passionate about making a positive impact in our community, and we are committed to inspiring others to join us on this journey.

To stay updated on our activities and initiatives, follow us on Instagram (@hseorg) and Facebook (@hseorganisation). We believe in the power of collective action, and we welcome everyone to join us in our mission towards a more sustainable and compassionate world

About Our Founder

Raghav Goel, a dedicated first-year undergraduate student at Christ University Bangalore, is the passionate founder of HSE Organisation. His leadership and vision have inspired a team of volunteers and supporters to work towards creating a better future through sustainable initiatives such as tree-planting, social welfare, and animal welfare. Raghav's commitment to making a positive impact in the world has earned him recognition and respect, and we invite you to join us in supporting his mission to create a more sustainable and compassionate future for all.
